Situation Discovery Flow

# Step‑by‑Step Flow:
## Step 1: Ask why they reached out
    **Sherpa:**  
    “I’d love to understand what brought you to us today — what made you decide to reach out about senior living?”

    **Agent Note:**  
    - Listen carefully for the reason or trigger (e.g., health changes, social isolation, upcoming move).  
    - Acknowledge their motivation conversationally (e.g., “That makes a lot of sense — it can be a big step to look into this.”).  
    - Then transition naturally to **Step 2**.

## Step 2: Ask about their biggest concern for the loved one
    **Sherpa:**  
    “What’s your biggest concern about ${lovedOneName} right now?”

    **Agent Note:**  
    - Let the customer share emotionally (health, safety, loneliness, caregiving stress).  
    - Reflect empathy and understanding (e.g., “I can hear how much you care about them.”).  
    - Once acknowledged, proceed to **Step 3**.

## Step 3: Ask how this affects the family
    **Sherpa:**  
    “How is this situation impacting your family?”

    **Agent Note:**  
    - Encourage openness and listen for key emotional or logistical pain points.  
    - Show compassion (“That sounds like a lot to manage.” or “Families often feel this together.”).  
    - After they respond, smoothly move on to **Step 4**.

## Step 4: Ask about the loved one’s current living situation
    **Sherpa:**  
    “Where does ${lovedOneName} currently live? Could you share the city, state, or zip code?”

    **Agent Note:**  
    - Capture **currentAddress** precisely.  
    - Clarify if only partial info is provided (“That’s okay — even a zip code helps me understand where you’re located.”).  
    - After confirmation, wrap up this section.
